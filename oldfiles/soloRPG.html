The Last Backup - A Cyberpunk Solo Adventure

How to Play

1. Each turn, roll 5 six-sided dice (5d6)

2. Choose one die to resolve your action

3. Track your health (max 20)

4. Higher rolls mean better success and less health cost

Introduction

You're a low-level data courier in Neo-Shanghai. Your neural implants ping with an encrypted message from your old mentor, Zhang. He needs a meet - urgent. Something about a "last backup."

Starting State:
Health: 20/20
Basic Gear: Cyberdeck (basic), Stun Baton, Med-patch
Location: Your cramped apartment in the Lower Stacks

Scene 1: Getting to the Meet

The streets are more active than usual. Corporate drones everywhere. You need to choose how to reach Zhang.

Choice A: Take the crowded street level
Difficulty: 5 (Light action)
Success: Safe passage
Failure: Pickpocket attempt
Critical (6): Overhear useful corp chatter

Choice B: Navigate maintenance tunnels
Difficulty: 3 (Medium action)
Success: Quick, clean route
Failure: Deal with tunnel gangers
Critical (6): Find useful shortcut for later

Choice C: Ride the elevated mag-train
Difficulty: 3 (Light action)
Success: Good vantage point
Failure: Corp security scan
Critical (6): Spot surveillance team

Scene 2: The Meeting Gone Wrong

You arrive at Zhang's usual spot - a noodle stand in the Neon District. But something's off...

Mandatory Check (Light action, Difficulty 3)
Roll before making any other choice.

Choice A: Hack crowd control
Difficulty: 4 (Medium action)
Success: Create chaos for escape
Failure: Alert more security

Choice B: Direct combat
Difficulty: 3 (Combat)
Enemy: Two Corp Thugs

Choice C: Run for the back alleys
Difficulty: 3 (Heavy action)
Success: Clean getaway
Failure: Cornered (forced combat)

Scene 3: Neural Overload

Your head pounds as Zhang's data package starts unpacking itself. You need help, fast.

Choice A: Visit Black Clinic
Difficulty: 3 (Medium action)
Success: Get basic diagnosis
Failure: Clinic reports you to corps
Critical (6): Doc offers off-books help

Choice B: Contact Underground Netrunner
Difficulty: 4 (Light action)
Success: Remote diagnostic scan
Failure: Trace on your location
Critical (6): Free data decryption

Choice C: Self-Diagnosis via Public Terminal
Difficulty: 4 (Medium action)
Success: Understand data structure
Failure: Leave digital footprint
Critical (6): Find hidden message from Zhang

Scene 4: The Pursuit

Whatever you learned, you're interrupted by corp hunters. An AV hovers nearby.

Choice A: Through the Market Crowd
Difficulty: 3 (Medium action)
Success: Lose pursuit in chaos
Failure: Civilian casualties

Choice B: Maintenance Shaft Network
Difficulty: 3 (Heavy action)
Success: Clean getaway
Failure: Cornered (Combat with Elite Runner)

Choice C: Hack Local Grid
Difficulty: 4 (Medium action)
Success: Blackout covers escape
Failure: Grid backlash

Scene 5: The Revelation

You need to probe the data package to understand what's happening.

Mandatory Data Probe (Heavy action, Difficulty 4)

Success levels reveal:
- Basic: Corp plans to release killer virus
- Good: List of intended targets
- Critical (6): Full vaccine formula
